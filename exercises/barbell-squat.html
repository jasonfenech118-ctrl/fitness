Barbell Back Squat

Exercise demonstration video

Instructions

  1. 1Position the barbell on your upper back/traps.
  2. 2Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  3. 3Lower your body by bending at the knees and hips.
  4. 4Descend until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  5. 5Drive through your heels to stand back up.

Pro Tips

Keep your knees tracking over your toes.
Maintain a neutral spine throughout.
Depth is important — aim for at least parallel.
